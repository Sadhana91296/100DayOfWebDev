Center Align element

To horizontally center a block element, use margin:auto;
The element will take up of specified width,and remaining space will equally distributed on both side

Center align text

To center the text use text-align:center;

Center an image

To set an image center make left and right margin:auto and display:block

Left and right align using position -

To align the block left or right use position:absolute and right/left:0px



Left or right align using float-

Use float:left or right to align left or right
